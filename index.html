Dr. Md Amiruzzaman is an assistant professor at West Chester University. Prior to coming to West Chester University, he worked as a computer programmer for almost 10 years for several companies (both nationally and internationally), also he was an assistant professor at Kent State University. Dr. Amiruzzaman completed his Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science from National University, and has completed three Master’s degree in Computer Engineering, Computer Science, and Technology. He worked as a research assistant at both Sejong University and Korea University and taught at both National University and Korea University. His research interests include visual analytics of urban data, data mining, deep learning, and data hiding. He received his Ph.D. degrees from Kent State University in 2016, 2019, and in 2021 respectively.
