I am an Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Science at <a href ="https://www.wcupa.edu/">West Chester University</a>. Prior to joining WCU, I worked as a software developer for almost 10 years for several companies. I have also held the position of Assistant Professor at <a href ="https://www.kent.edu/">Kent State University</a>.

I have completed my Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science from <a href ="https://www.nu.ac.bd/">National University</a>. Along with that, I have completed five Master's degrees with my major in Computer Engineering in 2008 from <a href ="https://eng.sejong.ac.kr/index.do">Sejong University</a>, Computer Science in 2011 from <a href ="https://www.kent.edu/">Kent State University</a> (also, partly at <a href ="http://korea.edu/mbshome/mbs/en/index.do">Korea University</a>) and Technology in 2015, also from <a href ="https://www.kent.edu/">Kent State University</a>, a Master's in Cybersecurity in 2023 from <a href ="https://www.gatech.edu/">Georgia Institute of Technology</a>, and a Master's in Applied Statistics in 2025 from <a href ="https://www.wcupa.edu/">West Chester University</a>. I received my Ph.D. degrees from <a href ="https://www.kent.edu/">Kent State University</a> in 2016 (Mathematics Edu), 2019 (Evaluation and Measurement) and 2021 (Computer Science).

In the past, I have worked as a Research Assistant at <a href ="https://eng.sejong.ac.kr/index.do">Sejong University</a> and <a href ="http://korea.edu/mbshome/mbs/en/index.do">Korea University</a>. Alongwith that, I gained the opportunity to teach at both <a href ="https://www.nu.ac.bd/">National University</a> and <a href ="http://korea.edu/mbshome/mbs/en/index.do">Korea University</a>. My research interests include Visual Analytics of urban data, Data Mining, Machine Learning, Deep Learning and Data Hiding.
